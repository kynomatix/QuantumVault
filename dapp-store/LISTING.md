## Long description  *(max ~4000 chars)*
QuantumVault is a Solana-native platform for automated perpetual futures trading. Deploy
and manage trading bots that run your strategies around the clock, turn TradingView alerts
into live trades, and track every position, PnL figure, and account-health metric in real
time.

Key features:
- Automated bot trading — deploy perpetual futures bots with hands-free execution.
- Real-time tracking — live positions, PnL, margin and account-health metrics.
- TradingView webhooks — convert your alerts into live trades automatically.
- Signal marketplace — publish your own signal bots or subscribe to others, with profit
  sharing for creators.
- QuantumLab backtesting — test and optimize Pine Script strategies before going live.
- Secure key management — server-managed wallets with encrypted keys under your control.
- Telegram notifications — configurable trade and account alerts.

Connect your Solana wallet and automate your perp trading from your phone.
